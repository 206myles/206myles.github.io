My name is Myles Black

Hi There! I'm Myles Black, from Seattle. I'm currently a student at The Tech Academy in Renton, Washington and also a developer at Impulse Media Group. I have over a year of experience creating websites, games and apps in languages like HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Python, C#, MySQL and MongoDB, however I feel most comfortable in JavaScript environments.

I graduated from a small private highschool in the heart of downtown Seattle better known as O'dea Highschool in 2016. From there I spent some time studying business management at the University of Washington and Bellevue College. It was at Bellevue where I realized my development hobby to be a passion, and from there I began to take my studies extremely serious. Since April 2017 I have dedicated a minimum of 30 hours a week to learning and developing. My entire journey has been a blast and I look forward to joining your team!
